PropIQ is an AI-powered platform that gives Bangalore property owners a single place to track their portfolio, get instant valuations, understand the market, and take action — all backed by a conversational AI copilot.

The Problem

Bangalore's property market is big,
opaque, and fragmented.

₹8L Cr

56+
Distinct micro-markets, each with wildly different price dynamics

Good tools for individual owners to track and understand their portfolio

Property owners today manage everything manually — spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, broker calls. There's no single source of truth for what their properties are actually worth, no easy way to compare markets, and no smart assistant to help them make decisions.

02 / 07

What We Built

Six features. One platform.

🏠

Portfolio Tracker

Add properties, track purchase price vs. AI-estimated current value, see total returns and appreciation across your entire portfolio.

🧮

AI Valuation

Get instant estimates for any Bangalore property based on locality data, BHK, sqft, and comparable properties — no broker needed.

📈

Market Analytics

Live price-per-sqft tables for 56 localities, sortable rankings, price distribution charts, and city-wide benchmarks.

🏪

P2P Marketplace

Browse and list properties for sale or rent, with an AI Fair Price Badge that flags if an asking price is above or below market.

🛡️

RERA Verification

Instant fuzzy-search across 50 Karnataka RERA-registered projects. Check registration status before investing.

🤖

AI Copilot

A floating chat assistant that answers questions about your portfolio, finds the best localities, and can create listings — using real data.
